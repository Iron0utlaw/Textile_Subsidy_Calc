Important Definitions

Gross Fixed Capital Investment (GFCI)

The total money a company spends to build or grow its factory (except land cost).

  • Includes factory buildings, machinery, tools, electricity setup, pollution control
  • Only items purchased during the project period count
  • Land cost is excluded from GFCI

Eligible Fixed Capital Investment (eFCI)

The part of GFCI that the government will accept when giving subsidies.

  • Includes new buildings (never used before)
  • Admin buildings (offices) are eligible
  • Cost counted as lower of actual cost or government-approved rates
  • Rented buildings don't count (except GIDC sheds)
  • Worker dormitories are eligible for eFCI
