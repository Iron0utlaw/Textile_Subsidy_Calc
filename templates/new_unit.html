New Unit Eligibility Information

New Unit Requirements

A new unit refers to a completely new textile manufacturing facility or unit.

  • Must be a completely new setup (not an expansion of existing unit)
  • Capital subsidy is only available for new machinery and for more than 10 years old machine (Imported)
  • Interest subsidy applies only for term loans for new machinery
  • Term Loan must be sanctioned within 1 year from Date of Commercial Production
  • Subsidies are disbursed after commencement of production
